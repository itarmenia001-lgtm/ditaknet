# DitakNet Brand Strategy

## 1. Executive Recommendation

Use **DitakNet** as the public product brand.

It is short, memorable, Armenian-rooted, and broad enough to grow beyond a single monitoring server into a full network visibility, discovery, support, licensing, reporting, topology, portal, agent, and future managed-services platform.

Recommended direction:

- Brand name: **DitakNet**
- Armenian spelling: **ԴիտակՆեթ**
- Core category: local network visibility and monitoring platform
- Best slogan: **See your network clearly**
- Armenian slogan: **Տեսեք ձեր ցանցը պարզ**
- Russian slogan: **Видьте свою сеть ясно**
- Best logo direction: minimal **D + lens + connected nodes**
- Best domain strategy: secure `ditaknet.am`; also try `ditaknet.com` or `getditaknet.com`

## 2. Brand Meaning

`Դիտակ` means a tool for seeing, observing, watching, or keeping something under view. `Net` connects the name to networks, IT infrastructure, connected devices, and digital systems.

Together, **DitakNet** means a practical tool for seeing what is happening inside a local network. The name communicates visibility, trust, local awareness, safe scanning, reliability, and professional IT support.

## 5. Logo Concepts

1. **D + lens + nodes**
   - Icon idea: the letter `D` shaped like a lens with connected points.
   - Meaning: observation plus network structure.
   - Visual style: minimal geometric app mark.
   - Color suggestion: deep blue with green node accent.
   - Works best for: website logo, favicon, dashboard sidebar, app icon.

2. **Eye + network pulse**
   - Icon idea: simple eye outline with a small pulse and node.
   - Meaning: visibility and alerts.
   - Visual style: clean line mark, not surveillance-heavy.
   - Color suggestion: blue/cyan with green pulse.
   - Works best for: marketing site and social avatar.

3. **Shield + signal circle**
   - Icon idea: shield containing a radar ring.
   - Meaning: trusted local scanning and business readiness.
   - Visual style: solid icon with simple internal geometry.
   - Color suggestion: navy shield, cyan ring, green check.
   - Works best for: enterprise-facing pages.

4. **Radar / discovery ring**
   - Icon idea: circular radar sweep with discovered nodes.
   - Meaning: automatic discovery and local awareness.
   - Visual style: strong at small sizes.
   - Color suggestion: blue ring with green active dots.
   - Works best for: Discovery module and app launcher.

5. **Armenian-inspired geometric mark**
   - Icon idea: restrained Armenian geometric structure forming a network point.
   - Meaning: Armenian-built technology without decorative excess.
   - Visual style: minimal, professional, not ornamental.
   - Color suggestion: deep blue with a careful amber accent.
   - Works best for: brand storytelling.

Final recommendation: **D + lens + connected nodes** because it is flexible, simple, and works as a favicon.
